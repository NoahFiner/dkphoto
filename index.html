about us

We are Day and Knight Photography, a collaboration between Noah Finer, Cormac Dowling, and Stephanie Zhang to supply a professional, affordable, and intimate portrait experience.

noah finer

Noah Finer is an award-winning photographer, designer, and web developer. After taking a photography class in his sophomore year to expand upon his previous talents, he continued to learn whatever was possible about it. He has since then had the honor to take over @universetoday for a day for his astrophotography, sold prints on bohowallart.com, has been featured on 34 different Instagram hubs, and has earned his bearded dragon, Spiky1, bearded dragon of the month through his photography. After massive experience in portraiture from being the media captain of the robotics team and a photographer for the Royal Banner, he would love to be chosen to take your senior photos!
