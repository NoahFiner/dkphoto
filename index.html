about us

We are Day and Knight Photography, a collaboration between Noah Finer, Cormac Dowling, and Stephanie Zhang to supply a professional, affordable, and intimate portrait experience.

noah finer

Noah Finer is an award-winning photographer, designer, and web developer, along with the founder of Day and Knight Photography. After taking a photography class in his sophomore year at Fairview, he continued to learn whatever was possible about cameras. He has since then taken over @universetoday for a day for his astrophotography, sold prints on bohowallart.com and individually, been featured on 40+ different Instagram hubs, and won Spiky1 the Bearded Dragon of the Month award through professional-grade photos. After massive experience in portraiture from being the media captain of the robotics team, a photographer for the Royal Banner, and the future NHS photographer, he would love to be chosen to take your senior photos!

cormac dowling

Cormac Dowling has been taking photographs for over two years. He began in his Junior year photography class, and he later joined his school's local newspaper, the Royal Banner, for photojournalism. He is now head photographer for the Royal Banner and his photographs are featured in each issue of the school paper. He also has taken many pictures for the school yearbook, as well as photos for a national magazine. He was recently invited to attend two upcoming national conferences to represent Colorado for youth photojournalism. Along with his photojournalism, he enjoys photographing nature and his dogs. He is excited for the experience of working with fellow students. Go Knights!!
